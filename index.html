About Dr Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He became one of India's most respected scientists and served as the President of India from 2002 to 2007. He is popularly known as the Missile Man of India for his work on missile development.
